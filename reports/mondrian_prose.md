## Stratified (Mondrian) conformal: overconfidence is structured, not uniform

Splitting the conformal audit by stratum shows that pooled FDR control masks a **structured** failure rather than a uniform one — the miscalibration concentrates in identifiable regions of the map.

**Which strata break.** Using the held-out DepMap co-essentiality referee (calibrated as a two-component mixture: true CORUM complexes are co-essential at rate h1 = 0.50, random decoys at h0 = 0.06), pooled conformal certification at q = 0.10 stays close to target for edges within **small (3–5 member) and medium (6–8 member) complexes** — estimated held-out FDR 0.12 (marginally above target) and 0.08 (below target) — but **breaks badly in large complexes (9 members): held-out FDR ≈ 0.65** (bootstrap 95% CI 0.18–1.0, n = 19 certified). That is more than 3× the pooled-overall held-out FDR (0.20) and 6× the nominal q. The referee is only mildly size-confounded (co-essential fraction of *all* candidate edges is 0.16/0.16/0.12 across small/medium/large), so the large-complex FDR is a real effect, not a sensitivity artifact.

**Independent confirmation via stratum recalibration.** All certifications fall in the top AF-M score tertile. When we run genuine Mondrian recalibration there — drawing conformal p-values against *score-matched* calibration decoys instead of the full pool — the certified count drops from **132 to 78 (a 1.7× pooled inflation)**, and the high-tertile held-out FDR rises from 0.20 to 0.26. Pooled conformal borrows easy, low-score decoys from other strata, so its p-values in the hard high-score regime are anti-conservative.

**Data-availability caveats (confound-control rule).** Only two defensible strata were computable map-wide: AF-M **score tertiles** (the sole *recalibratable* stratum — calibration decoys carry `score` but no complex label) and the paper's own **complex size** (referee-only). **Homo- vs hetero-dimer is degenerate** (0/1666 candidates are homodimers). **IDR fraction, MSA depth (Neff), and interface size are unavailable map-wide** — per-protein sequences and MSAs exist only for the single KANSL nominee in `data/structures/`, and Table 5 tabulates no interface-PAE or pDockQ2 — so those strata are honestly reported as not computable rather than approximated.

**Wording guardrail.** Held-out FDR is an **upper bound** (CORUM is incomplete; some certified decoys may be genuine interactions). The finding is that large-complex certifications are **not supported at q = 0.10 under distribution-free, stratum-matched calibration**, not that those complexes are false.
